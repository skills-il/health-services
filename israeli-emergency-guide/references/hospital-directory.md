## Notes on Hospital Selection

- **In an emergency**, call 101 and let MDA determine the appropriate hospital. Do not drive to a hospital yourself if the situation is life-threatening.
- **For non-life-threatening urgent care**, Terem clinics offer shorter wait times and lower costs than hospital ERs.
- **All hospitals** are required to treat emergency patients regardless of kupat cholim membership or ability to pay.
- **Kupat cholim affiliation** may affect which hospital is preferred for non-emergency care (each kupat cholim has agreements with specific hospitals).
- **Specialists**: If you need a specific specialty not listed at a nearby hospital, ask your kupat cholim for a referral to the appropriate facility.
