# Israeli Elder Care Benefits Reference (2026)

## Old-Age Pension (Kiztavat Zikna) -- Bituach Leumi

### Retirement Age (2026)
- Men: 67
- Women: 63.5 (rising by 3 months/year to reach 65 by 2032)

### Monthly Amounts (January 2026)
- Basic individual pension: 1,838 NIS
- Spouse supplement: 924 NIS
- Child supplement: 581 NIS (first 2 children only)
- Health insurance deduction: -237 NIS (individual), -340 NIS (couple)

### Income Test
- Under 70: pension is means-tested (income from work reduces pension)
- 70+: everyone receives pension regardless of income
- Income supplement (hashlamat hachnasa) available for low-income elderly

## Long-Term Care Benefit (Gimlat Siud) -- Bituach Leumi

### Eligibility
- Reached retirement age
- Lives at home (NOT in a nursing institution)
- Needs help with ADL (activities of daily living)
- Passes assessment by Bituach Leumi assessor

### Benefit Levels

| Level | ADL Points | Weekly Hours | With Foreign Worker | Typical ADL Profile |
|-------|-----------|-------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| 1 | 2.5-3 | 5.5 | 5.5 | Needs help with 1-2 ADL areas |
| 2 | 3.5-4.5 | 10 | 10 | Needs help with 3-4 ADL areas |
| 3 | 5-6 | 17 | 14 | Needs help with most ADL areas |
| 4 | 6.5-7.5 | 21 | 18 | Needs help with nearly all ADL areas |
| 5 | 8-9 | 26 | 22 | Dependent in most ADL areas |
| 6 | 9.5-10.5 | 30 | 26 | Fully dependent in all ADL areas |

### Services Included
- Home caregiver visits
- Adult day care center attendance
- Laundry service
- Absorbent products (incontinence supplies)

### Exclusions
- Does NOT cover nursing home costs
- Does NOT cover foreign caregiver salary directly
- Stops when person moves to a nursing institution

### Application
- Phone: *6050 or local Bituach Leumi branch
- Assessor visits home to evaluate functional level
- Decision typically within 30-60 days

## Private Nursing Care Insurance (Bituach Siudi)

### Key Rules
- Purchased ONLY through kupot cholim
- CANNOT enroll after age 65
- Free for minors under 18
- Lower premiums at younger enrollment ages

### What It Provides
- Monthly cash benefit when person becomes ADL-dependent
- Amount varies by plan and kupat cholim
- Can be received simultaneously with gimlat siud
- Typically covers nursing home or home caregiver costs

### The Four Kupot Cholim
- Clalit Mushlam/Platinum
- Maccabi Zahav/Sheli
- Meuhedet Adif/Magen
- Leumit Zahav/Kesef

Each offers different siudi plans with different coverage levels and premiums.
